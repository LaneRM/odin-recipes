Lasagna

See that? That's lasagna. It's got meat, cheese, and some other stuff. Try it, you'll like it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according the recipe.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
